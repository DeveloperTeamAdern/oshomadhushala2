About Us

Osho Madhushala is a place for meditation and where people can meet their true selves. Osho Madhushala was founded in 1980 by swami Chandermoli Jii. The Osho Madhushala Foundation is a part of Osho Glimpse which is licensed under Osho International Foundation Switzerland. Here we organize daily sessions from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM for osho meditations. Currently Osho Madhushala is lead by Aantar Muni. He is the facilator of Osho meditation, Osho hypnosis and Osho no mind meditative therapy from Osho International Foundation Switzerland. We do also organise some perodic festivals for meditation purposes with daily meditation.
